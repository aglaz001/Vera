# Vera Site Vision & Roadmap

## 1. Vision
Vera is the "Editorial Archivist" of ESG reporting. It provides calm authority and credible compliance for SMEs in Africa.

**Stitch Project ID:** `909829974979427571`

## 2. Global Navigation
- **Dashboard**: Overview of ESG scores and progress.
- **Data Entry**: Tabs for Environmental, Social, and Governance pillars.
- **Compliance**: Gap analysis against frameworks (GRI, CSRD).
- **Report Editor**: AI-assisted narrative generation.
- **Preview & Export**: Final report generation.
- **Settings**: Team and Organization management.
